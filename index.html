About Us

Geeta Computer started back in 2018 as a small repair counter with one workbench and a simple promise: fix it properly, explain it honestly, and charge fairly. Word spread, and over the years we grew into a full computer and CCTV service centre trusted by homes, shops, and offices across town.

Whether it's a slow laptop, a custom PC build, a printer that won't behave, or a full CCTV setup for your shop, the same technicians who answer your call are the ones who do the work. No call centres, no jargon — just straight answers and dependable repairs you can come back to.
